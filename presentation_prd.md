# PRD: StudySpot Presentation Generator

**Core Objective:** To present StudySpot as a revolutionary real-time coordination hub for MIT-WPU students that solves campus navigation and collaborative friction.

---

### Slide 1: Title & Vision
- **App Name:** StudySpot
- **Tagline:** "Your Live Campus Coordination Hub."
- **Focus:** Real-time visibility, collaborative deep work, and AI-powered learning.

### Slide 2: The Problem (The "Why")
- **Campus Friction:** Students often wander for hours looking for free study spots or groups studying their specific subjects.
- **Inertia:** Hard to find collaborators for niche subjects (Engineering Physics, Advanced Calc) in huge library spaces.
- **Safety & Privacy:** No way to host private, moderated study groups in public campus areas without being interrupted.

### Slide 3: The Solution (About the App)
- **Live Feed:** Real-time dashboard of every active study group on campus.
- **Visual Intelligence:** "SeatMapper" technology provides a literal blueprint overlay of the library, showing exactly where pins are dropped.
- **Real-Time Workspace:** Instant task-syncing, resource sharing, and moderation controls for every session.

### Slide 4: Tech Stack & Features
- **Frontend/Backend:** Vite, React, TailwindCSS, and Supabase (Auth, DB, & Realtime).
- **Core Features:**
  - **Host Moderation:** Approve/Deny/Kick functionality to maintain quality.
  - **Shared Progress:** Real-time task checklists that sync across all joined members.
  - **Global Notifications:** Background polling to ensure you never miss a collaborator's request to join.

### Slide 5: Why We’re Unique (The "X-Factor")
- **AI Integration (Internal & External):**
  - **Global AI Assistant:** Parses massive session lists to "Auto-Join" you based on simple chat requests.
  - **In-Session AI Tutor:** Powered by Groq (Llama 3.1). Context-aware logic that generates roadmaps, explains subject concepts, and creates sample exam questions based on *this specific* group's tasks.
- **Granular Privacy:** Time-limited sessions with "Waiting Rooms" for private groups.

### Slide 6: The "Impact" (Student Life)
- **Time Reclaimed:** Redefining study efficiency by eliminating the "search" phase of studying.
- **Peer Networking:** Facilitating ad-hoc peer-to-peer tutoring for difficult subjects.
- **Democratized Learning:** Bringing professional-grade coordination tools directly to the library floor.
- **Scalability:** Built for MIT-WPU today; ready for any university campus tomorrow.
